Education/Career Goals

Right now I'm working on a bachelor's degree in web development. I'm also pursuing a minor in Japanese Language. My short term goal is to get an internship and expand my skills in the workplace. I have a lot to learn about the industry, but considering where I was when I started my degree I'm very proud of my progress thus far.

My short term career goals are to get more practical industry experience, gain more experience working in a team, and improve my resume and skillset. I'm eager to expand my technical knowledge and get comfortable with more languages and frameworks. I like doing design-oriented tasks and making websites that look nice, but I like doing so from a developer's lens with CSS, rather than just pure design.

In the long term, I would love to live abroad and do my own freelance work. One part of web development that really appeals to me is the inherent flexibility and mobility. I'm open to different paths though. I would love to expand my coding expertise to include game development in the future and work on my own projects in that department as well.
